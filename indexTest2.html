Setting Ourselves Free: A Selection of Readings, Resources, and Sounds.

A non-exhaustive source list exemplifying how Black people have done and continue to actively take the principle role in freeing one’s self and one’s community. The selected works featured in this source list convey the distinct spirit of triumph - while the conditions we continue to liberate ourselves from are insidious and oppressive, collectively we continue to carve spaces for uninhibited joy and celebration.

Resources curated by the Black Artist Research Space.
